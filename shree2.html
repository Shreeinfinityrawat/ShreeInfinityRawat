Life isn’t a straight path—it’s more like a patchwork of detours, delays, and unexpected beauty. We plan for clarity, but often grow in the confusion. The lessons don’t always come with explanations, but they always come with purpose.

Some days feel like poetry, others like puzzles. We chase meaning in moments and find it in hindsight. It’s easy to overlook the quiet victories—waking up, holding on, starting again. But they matter. They shape us.
